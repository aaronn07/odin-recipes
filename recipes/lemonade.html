Lemonade

A refresh drink, lemonade

Now, to accompany your taco or your sandwich lets make an easy drink but that is very tasty

Ingredients

    • Lemons
      Sugar
      Water
      Glass
      Ice

    • Steps

    • Put 2 spons of sugar on a glass (a glass with 600 ml aproximately)
      Mix it with a half of water in the glass, water must be warm but not hot
      Cut 2 lemons and squeeze onto the lemonade
      Fill the rest with cold water, but not to the top
      Mix it
      Add some ice cubes
      Enjoy
