Lemonade

A refresh drink, lemonade

Now, to accompany your taco or your sandwich lets make an easy drink but that is very tasty

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Glass
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Put 2 spons of sugar on a glass (a glass with 600 ml aproximately)
  2. Mix it with a half of water in the glass, water must be warm but not hot
  3. Cut 2 lemons and squeeze onto the lemonade
  4. Fill the rest with cold water, but not to the top
  5. Mix it
  6. Add some ice cubes
  7. Enjoy
