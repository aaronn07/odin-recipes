Sandwich

Today we will make a simple but delicious sandiwch, this will be so easy and its enjoyable with chips and the sauce of your prefference

Ingredients

    • Bread
      Lettuce
      Tomatos
      Cheese
      Jam
      Mayonnaise
      Ketchup
      Mustard

    • Steps

    • Mix on a bowl two spons of each sauce (ketchup, mustard and mayonnaise)
      Spread the sauce into one face from both pieces of bread
      Add on the spreaded sauce face of the slice bread: jam, cheese, tomatos and lettuce
      Close it with the other slice bread
      Enjoy
