Sandwich

A simple sandwich

Today we will make a simple but delicious sandiwch, this will be so easy and its enjoyable with chips and the sauce of your prefference

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese
  • Jam
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix on a bowl two spons of each sauce (ketchup, mustard and mayonnaise)
  2. Spread the sauce into one face from both pieces of bread
  3. Add on the spreaded sauce face of the slice bread: jam, cheese, tomatos and lettuce
  4. Close it with the other slice bread
  5. Enjoy
