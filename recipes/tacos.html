Tacos al Pastor

This tacos were made with Pastor meat which consists on a pork meat marinated in a mix of ingredients, and also have some vegtables like avocados, onions, and cilantro

No doubt you should try this recipe on your own, its really cheap and efficiently for a family

Ingredients

  • Pork meat
  • Marinated sauce
  • Tortillas
  • Onions
  • Avocados
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork meat on the sauce for an a half hour
  2. Cut into small pieces, like cubes
  3. Cook it in a frying pan, fryer, grill, griddle, etc. Make sure it doesnt dry at all.
  4. Warm the tortillas on a microwave
  5. Put the meat on the tortillas
  6. Cut some onions into small pieces, and do the same with the cilantro and avocados
  7. Put the vegetables on the tacos
  8. Pray and enjoy
